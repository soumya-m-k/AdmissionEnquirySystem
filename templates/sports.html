NCC

Promoting ideals of selfless services among the youth.

AIMS

1. To develop qualities of character, courage, comradeship, discipline, leadership, secular outlook, spirit of adventure, sportsmanship and ideals of selfless services among the youth of the country to make them useful citizens.

2. To create a human resource of organized, trained and motivated youth, to provide leadership in all walks of life and always be available for the service of the nation.

3.To provide a suitable environment to motivate the youth to take up a career in the armed forces.

NSS

NSS develops service mentality, discipline, leadership qualities, professional dignity, cooperation, fraternity, secularism, unity and integrity, responsibility, and personality development. Students learn by self-experience. Altogether N.S.S. means "Education through Service and Service through Education."

NSS Programes

1. Protection and development of environment.

2. Health and family welfare programmes.

3. Literacy, Cultural and Adventurous activities.

4. Adoption of villages for all round development.

5. Survey of literacy, social and economic status of villagers.

6. Plantation and watering.

7. Construction of roads, playgrounds.

8. Women empowerment programmes.

9. Protection of old monuments.

10. Participation of volunteers in leadership camps at University and other levels.
