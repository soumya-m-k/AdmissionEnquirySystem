Hostel Facility

HOSTEL

The college has hostels for both male and female students.The hostel for girls is located beside the college amidst a glistening panoramic view.The boys' hostel is situated outside the college campus. Both hostels provide a comfortable and conducive atmosphere to develop the innate abilities of inmates.Indoor and outdoor facilities for sports and cultural activities are available in both hostels.
