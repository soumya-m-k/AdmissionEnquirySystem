Library Facility

LIBRARY

The library is well-stocked with a collection of more than 60,000 books. Besides, it has subscription to 12 newspapers, 41 periodicals and magazines and about 40 journals. NewGenLib software is used for Library automation and OPAC is provided for searching. Access to e-Journals is available through INFLIBNET consortium.

Facilities Provide

our library OPAC

1. Each student is provided with an Identity Card and Borrower's Card. Books are issued on Borrower's Card on a fortnightly basis, and two books are lent additionally to SC/ST, Merit, NCC, NSS and Differently abled students up to semester exams.

2. Barcode facility is provided.

3. OPAC (Online Public Access Catalogue) search facility is provided to the students and staff to enable them easy access to the library's resources.

4. Library provides lending, reference, photocopy and interlibrary loan facility to the students.

5. The Librarian ensures the purchasing of current titles in consultation with the respective department. Suggestions of students are also considered while purchasing books.

6. Separate section is provided for competitive examination and career guidance books

7. Computers are available in the library for OPAC services and access to e-journals.
